#### Summary of Arc Raiders the game

Arc raiders is an extraction shooter center around PvPvE. The gameplay loop consists of players loading into different maps in a post apolovalypse Italy and do quests, raid dilapitated structures, and fight robots called "ARC". They can additionally fight fellow player raiders for loot. As you continue through the game you gain experience and obtain skills that can help you upgrade the following skill tree.
